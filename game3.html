Bison Blocks

Game Description

Bison Blocks is a fun puzzle game where players control a powerful bison to move blocks, solve puzzles, and discover new levels. You'll face intricate mazes, strategic tasks, and engaging gameplay that will entertain players of all ages.

The game features colorful graphics, relaxing music, and a variety of unique mechanics. Each level brings more challenging puzzles that will test your logic and forward-thinking ability.

Game Rules

  • Main Objective: Move blocks and reach the exit of the maze. Solve puzzles with the fewest moves to earn more points.
  • Controls: Move the bison using arrow keys on the keyboard or gestures on the touchscreen. Push blocks by moving the bison in the right direction.
  • Types of Blocks:
    • Regular Blocks: Can be moved in all directions.
    • Heavy Blocks: Can only be moved in specific directions.
    • Breakable Blocks: Disappear after one move.
    • Dynamic Blocks: Move on their own after activation.
  • Obstacles and Mechanics:
    • Lava Pits: You cannot move blocks or the bison over them.
    • Buttons: Activate them to open hidden paths.
    • Portals: Teleport blocks or the bison to another part of the level.

      • Solo Mode: Solve puzzles at your own pace.
